Window Movement

  • <C-w><C-h> or <C-h> - move to the left window
  • <C-w><C-j> or <C-j> - move to the bottom window
  • <C-w><C-k> or <C-k> - move to the up window
  • <C-w><C-l> or <C-l> - move to the right window
